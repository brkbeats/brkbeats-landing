Next Steps

Download, install, and activate.

01

Check your email from Creem

Creem just sent you a purchase confirmation email. In that email, click the purple Download File link — this takes you to your Creem order page. If you don't see the email, check your spam folder.

02

Find your download and license key on the Creem order page

On the Creem order page, click SP-404MK2 Effects Controller under Orders on the left side. The right side will open showing everything you need.

🔑
License Key — your unique activation code. Copy it, you'll need it when you first launch the app.
⬇️
Files — click the download icon next to SP-404MK2 Effects.zip to get your installer.
📄
Inside the zip — Mac installer (.pkg), Windows installer (.exe), and the Start Here PDF guide.
03

Run the installer for your platform

Extract the zip and run the installer for your OS. Mac users see the Start Here PDF for Gatekeeper bypass instructions. Windows users see it for SmartScreen instructions. Both are a one-time step.

04

Launch and enter your license key

The first time you launch the app or plugin, you'll see an activation screen. Paste in the license key from your Creem order page.

05

Connect your SP-404MK2

Plug in via USB, turn on the hardware, and in the app's Options menu set both the MIDI Input and MIDI Output to SP-404MKII. Both directions must be set for bidirectional knob control to work.
